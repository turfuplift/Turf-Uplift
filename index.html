About Us

Turf Uplift is dedicated to maintaining the beauty and functionality of artificial turf. Founded by Louis Antonio Torres, our mission is to provide exceptional cleaning services that enhance the appearance and longevity of your property. Louis, originally from New Jersey, discovered his passion for artificial turf while serving his community in North Las Vegas. With a focus on meticulous care and customer satisfaction, Louis ensures that every plot of land we service is cleaned and presented in a way that leaves our customers delighted. Your property is one of your most significant investments, and we are here to help you keep it in pristine condition.

Why Our Services Matter

Your artificial turf deserves the best care, and that's exactly what we provide. A clean and well-maintained turf is not just about appearance—it’s about hygiene, safety, and longevity. Our services remove harmful debris, eliminate unpleasant odors, and ensure your turf remains a healthy and enjoyable space for your family and pets.

Pet Owners Rejoice! Pets love playing on turf, but they can also leave behind messes and odors. Our specialized cleaning process is safe for pets and tackles stubborn spots, leaving your turf fresh, odor-free, and safe for everyone to enjoy.

When you choose Turf Uplift, you're not just maintaining your property—you’re investing in a cleaner, greener, and healthier outdoor environment.
